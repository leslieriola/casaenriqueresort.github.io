About Casa Enrique Resort

Casa Enrique Resort is a home to San Miguel Foods Corp. Chicken farm managed and owned by Mam Ana Riola-Brodheim, where tasty chickens and egss were nurtured and grew as the food of the Filipino. Today, it is Pangasinan's jewel; an exceptional touristic accommodation and destination just off the coast of the bustling Mabini town. Here, luxurious comfort, breathtaking Filipino design, a protected natural environment, and the rich culture of the northern Philippines meet. Together, they create a relaxing, private retreat unlike any other in the Philippine archipelago.
